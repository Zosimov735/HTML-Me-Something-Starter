I've had quite a few jobs

Buckle up

Old Navy

Old Navy. The place where it all started. I was only 16 years old. They thought I was a great candidate for their Sale's Associate position. I was hired in October 2013 and worked there until June 2014 . A total of seven months.

MobileSpot

MobileSpot was a phone repair store that I worked at while also working at Old Navy. They fixed mobile devices and sold prepaid plans and overpriced phone cases. It was a valuable learning experience and fun for a seventeen year old to dabble in repairing the then flagship devices such as the iPhone 5s. I left there in June 2014 along with Old Navy

Salvatore's Pizza & Pasta

In August of 2014 I started working as a dishwasher at Salvatore's Pizza and Pasta and it was a fun job. I got trained in all the positions and managed the place until January.
